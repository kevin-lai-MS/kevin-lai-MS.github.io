當局者迷，旁觀者清

The player sees less clearly than the bystander.

I have been listening to some youtube lectures by Alan Watts,a guy populorized eastern philosphy for westerners. I found this situation, a western thinker explaining eastern philosphy, to be extremely intersting. Maybe because as a easterner, we do not lack in easterner explaining western philosphy. Or maybe because when I was growing up in the east, playing the game, participating actively in eastern society, I do not feel the underlining philosphy in the game. However while lisening to Alan Watts, I realized the awesome position I am in. I am a man of both worlds, growing up in both the East and the West, not reconized as a local anywhere. This position enable me to look at both worlds with a different prospective. Living in the West, listeing to westerners talking about the east, and while living in the East, listening to the easterner talks of the west.
